ABOUT ROBOLUTION

Igniting the Robotic Revolution

Robolution is the flagship national robotics competition organized by the MIST Robotics Club (MRC), one of the most active and prestigious robotics clubs in Bangladesh. Established in 2013 and functioning under the esteemed EECE department of MIST, MRC's mission has always been to spread knowledge, enthusiasm, and innovation in robotics across the country.

Following the tremendous success of its previous iterations in 2016, 2017, 2018, and 2019, MIST Robotics Club is proud to announce the next chapter: Robolution 5.0, set to unfold on November 28-29.
